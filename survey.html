Students Satisfaction Survey
To conduct student satisfaction survey (SSS) on teaching learning process during the academic year 2016-17 a questionnaire was designed by IQAC. This printed questionnaire was distributed to the UG students of final year. The questionnaire was prepared covering various aspects of Teaching Learning process, Library facilities, and college office and on general amenities.
5% students opined that classes held regularly with a positive answer. 72.3% students reported that teachers do take initiatives to improve quality of teaching and teacher’s behaviors toward students was very good. They help student in matters other than study. The institution provides multiple opportunities to learn and grow. Students are satisfied that teachers are able to identify the weaknesses and help them to overcome them.
80% of the students are satisfied with services provided by the college.
